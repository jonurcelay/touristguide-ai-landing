Pros and Cons of Budget Travel

Pros:

  • Save Money: Stretch your budget with free or low-cost activities.
  • Authentic Experiences: Discover hidden gems off the tourist track.
  • Flexibility: Pivot plans without expensive cancellations.

Cons:

  • Time Investment: Planning takes effort to find deals.
  • Limited Comfort: Budget options may mean basic accommodations.
  • Hidden Costs: Cheap transport can have unexpected fees.
